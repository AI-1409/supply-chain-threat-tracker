Description

Threat actors conducted a sophisticated phishing campaign targeting Rust crate maintainers under the guise of security vulnerability reports. Attackers lured maintainers to a credential harvesting site that mimicked GitHub's login interface, potentially compromising maintainer accounts and enabling future package supply chain attacks.
